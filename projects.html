My Projects

Welcome to my projects page a showcase of my growth, creativity, and technical exploration in web development. Throughout my coursework in CSCI 202: Dynamic Web Pages at Western Washington University, I have worked on a series of projects that blend design, interactivity, and problem-solving. Each project represents a step forward in mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON, while also reflecting my passion for crafting engaging and functional digital experiences. From the mood transforming aesthetics of Mood Transformation to the interactive narratives of Neon 42 - Chatbot Traveler, and from dynamic painting collections to practical solutions like Study Spot Finder, these projects highlight both innovation and technical precision. As I continue building my skill set, these works stand as milestones in my journey pushing boundaries, refining techniques, and embracing the challenges of modern web development. Feel free to explore, interact, and experience the creativity behind each project!
