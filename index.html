Achievement

Throughout my time in CSCI 202: Dynamic Web Pages, I engaged in hands-on web development, building interactive and dynamic projects that showcase both technical proficiency and creativity. From Mood Transformation to Neon 42 - Chatbot Traveler, I explored user-centered design, crafting intuitive and engaging digital experiences. My work on Painting Collection, Painting Animation, and Famous Paintings deepened my understanding of structured data and animation, allowing me to create visually compelling showcases. Additionally, projects like Study Spot Finder and Suggestions4Free reinforced my ability to develop practical solutions that address real-world needs. Through these efforts, I strengthened my foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON, gaining confidence in structuring, styling, and implementing meaningful digital experiences.

Acknowledgment

I am grateful for the opportunity to learn and grow throughout CSCI 202: Dynamic Web Pages.
